Mike's Homemade Pizza

Make this homemade pizza with basic ingredients, then top it with any of your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • dough
  • sauces
  • salami
  • ham
  • vegetables

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Make the dough: Dissolve yeast in lukewarm water in a small bowl. Let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes.
  3. Combine flour, shortening, and salt in a large bowl; add yeast mixture and stir until dough comes together. Knead dough on a lightly floured surface until smooth and elastic, about 8 minutes.
  4. Lightly oil a large bowl. Place dough into the bowl and turn to coat with oil. Cover with a damp cloth and let rise in a warm place until doubled in volume, about 45 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile, make the sauce: Heat oil in a small saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and saut√© until tender, about 5 minutes.
  6. Stir in tomato paste, water, salt, sugar, oregano, garlic powder, basil, marjoram, cumin, chili powder, red pepper flakes, and black pepper; simmer for 15 to 20 minutes.
  7. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  8. Divide dough in half. Roll each half into a 12-inch circle. Place dough onto two 12-inch pizza pans; cover with sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven until crusts are golden brown, about 20 minutes.
